In our current state misinformation is frequently shared and whether this is intentional or inadvertent it can have catastrophic results on the spread of COVID-19. Maximising public access to balanced, reliable, and up to date information is a critical success factor for authorities worldwide. Management of highly trusted information will have a vital role to play in the control of COVID-19. The COVID-19 Fact Check app has been built to facilitate an effective means of sharing information going forward. The application is an informal educational tool, which encourages users to become aware of the false or misleading information that they may find themselves coming across when partaking in online activity.
