Visualising Rainfall Data to Enhance Bangladesh’s Capacity for Risk Reduction in City Planning

Collaboration Team:

Maya Okada-Zalewski, Mohammad Amin Mohammadi, Heath McGregor

The focus of this research is on Bangladesh and the severity of their natural disasters. This involves two main sets of data, natural disaster levels and population which would be displayed according to each district. We collected data of Bangladesh’s monthly rainfall data from 1948-2014. Experimenting with ways to visualise it, we have plotted the data on a map and animated the visualisation according to its data. The images below show the first four iterations to achieve a better understanding of the best way to visualize the main factors.
